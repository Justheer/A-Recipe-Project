Gulab Jamun

Ingredient

  • Flour
  • Grated Paneer
  • Sooji/Rava

Recipe

Barfi

Ingredient

  • condensed milk
  • cup milk powder
  • 2 tablespoon ghee

Recipe

  1. Take a large bowl and add milk powder to it along with milk. Make a rigid dough using these ingredients.
  2. Take the dough and grate it in a bowl. Keep this grated dough aside for further usage.
  3. Add the grated dough to this pan with water. Mix well and stir in cardamom powder in the pan.
  4. Pour this prepared mixture into a greased tray and garnish it with almonds and pistachios. Let the mixtur.
  5. Serve!
